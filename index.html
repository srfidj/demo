Barcode vs. RFID at a Glance

1. Maintaining 99% Inventory Accuracy

Barcode

  • 600 items counted per hour
  • 52 audits annually required
  • Cost: $7,800 per 10,000 items
  • Limited to product SKUs tracking

RFID

  • 1,000 items counted per minute
  • Frequent, low-cost audits
  • Cost: $78 per 10,000 items
  • Serialized tags for individual tracking
